Welcome to the Chili Yield and Type Predictor

Use this application to predict the type of chili and the expected yield based on your input.

The Chilli Crop Predictor is a cutting-edge web application that leverages the power of machine learning to forecast the yield and quality of chilli crops. By analyzing a range of factors, including weather conditions, soil type, and farming practices, this innovative tool provides farmers with valuable insights to optimize their crop management strategies. One of the key factors that influences crop yield is the environment. Weather conditions, such as temperature, rainfall, and sunlight exposure, can significantly impact the growth and development of chilli crops. The Chilli Crop Predictor takes into account these environmental factors, as well as crop genotypic and phenotypic characteristics, to provide accurate yield predictions.

Type of Chillis

Sanam Chili

Sanam chili is a popular variety of chili pepper commonly grown in India. It is known for its high yield and is often used in cooking.

Teja Chili

Teja chili is a type of chili pepper known for its high heat and is often used in Indian cuisine. It is commonly grown in the southern regions of India.

334 Chili

334 chili is a hybrid variety of chili pepper known for its high yield and disease resistance. It is commonly grown in India and is used in a variety of dishes.

Byadgi Chili

Byadgi chili is a type of chili pepper commonly grown in the state of Karnataka, India. It is known for its high heat and is often used in cooking.

Ramnad Chili

Ramnad chili is a type of chili pepper commonly grown in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is known for its high heat and is often used in cooking.
